About Me

Who I am?

Hello! My name is Margret Schnirring. I'm a sports communication and media major at Miami University. I'm passionate about social media, sports, and creating engaging content. I love sharing my experiences with others, especially when it comes to my journey as a woman working in the sports industry.

Why I want to work in sports

I've always been drawn to sports, not only because of the excitement of the games but also the powerful stories behind each athlete and team. I want to be part of a team that brings those stories to life through media and social platforms, helping to inspire and engage fans around the world.

How I got to do what I am doing now

My journey started when I decided to pursue a degree in sports communication and media. During my time at Miami University, I got the opportunity to work closely with the athletics department, assisting with social media content creation and event promotion. I learned the ins and outs of the industry, built valuable skills in digital marketing, and developed a passion for telling stories that matter in the sports world. It has been an exciting journey so far, and I'm looking forward to the opportunities ahead!
