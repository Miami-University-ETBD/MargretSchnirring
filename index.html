My Journey in Sports at Miami University

Short Bio

Hi! I'm Margret Schnirring, a passionate sports lover with the privilege of working and learning in the world of sports at Miami University. As a girl in sports, my experience has been one of overcoming challenges, and embracing my role as a women in a male-dominated field. Throughout my journey, I have worked with diverse teams, learned from incredible mentors, and gained invaluable knowledge that I carry with me forever.

My journey is a story of passion, growth, and the power of breaking stereotypes.

Key Highlights of My Experience

  • Internship with the Miami University Athletics Department – Gained hands-on experience in creating an engaging atmosphere for all sports fans.
  • Running social media for various Miami sports teams – Worked with coaches, players, and colleagues to create content for Miami Redhawks social media pages to make viewers more interested about our sports teams.
  • Member of Women In Sports Leadership Club – Was apart of a club that were full of amazing and inspiring women wanting to take roles in the sports industry..
